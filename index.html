Help with the game

What is it?

This is a game of exploration, discovery, and adventure.

Try to survive as you build up your crew and gear. Can you discover the secret of the island?

How it works

Travel

To move around on the map, click it. You can click the little map or the big one. One click sends you in that direction for one hour. How far you go in that hour depends on many factors: your travel mode (you can change this), the terrain, the weather, how much stuff you are carrying, your health, and more.
